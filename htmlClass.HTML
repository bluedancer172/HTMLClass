HTML Class

Our HTML Class is all done online. We meet once a week through Google Meets, every Saturday at 8:30, and our classes range from 30 minutes to a max of 1 hour.

We also have a Google Classroom where all assignments are posted, and a google hangouts, so we all can interact with eachother if we need help or to help someone else.

What do we do?

In each class...

During each class, we start by (one by one) presenting our projects/proggress on our projects, and sometimes give constructive criticism to eachother. Next, we either going over a new topic or reviewing a old one. Our information was all found through w3schools .

Projects!

For the first few weeks, we worked on webpages that had a little bit of information about us(Basic facts, likes/dislikes,etc.). The new topics we leart during class would often be then used in our webpages. After we learned a few new things and answered any questions, we would improve our webpages, until we finalized them. Once our "All About Me!" Webpages were done, we moved on to creating a webpage for the class, that had everyone's wepages on it!
